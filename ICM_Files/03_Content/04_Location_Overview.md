# Roaring Pines Motorclub — Location Overview

## Address
**State Road 100 at Palatka Municipal Airport**
Palatka, Florida — Northeast Florida

---

## Why This Location

### Climate
- Northeast Florida = year-round driving season
- No seasonal shutdowns; track open all 12 months
- Warm winters, manageable summers for motorsport

### Aviation Access
- Adjacent to Palatka Municipal Airport (Kay Larkin Field)
- **Runway:** 6,000 ft → expanding to 7,000 ft
- Walking distance (<0.1 miles) to the track
- Modern FBO with private hangars on-site

## Local Attractions (within 10 miles)
- **Downtown Palatka** (3.5 mi): Restaurants, local brewery, shops, waterfront park with panoramic views of the St. Johns River
- **Palatka Golf Course** (4 mi): Donald Ross-designed, ranked top-30 municipal courses in the United States
- **Boathouse Marina** (6.5 mi): Full-service boat storage, world-class fishing on St. Johns River, 50 miles to Intracoastal Waterway, 55 miles to Atlantic Ocean

## Regional Leisure Ecosystem
The region pairs track performance with exceptional lifestyle options:
- Championship golf (TPC Ponte Vedra, Palatka Donald Ross course, World Equestrian Center area)
- Pristine fishing (St. Johns River, Atlantic, Intracoastal)
- Vibrant aviation scene (Palatka Airport FBO + Jacksonville Executive)
- Beaches: St. Augustine, Ponte Vedra, Daytona within 30–50 miles
- Dining, culture, nightlife: Jacksonville (65 mi), St. Augustine (30 mi)

Florida stands out as a premier motorsport destination, combining:

Roaring Pines sits at the center of this ecosystem.
