# Roaring Pines Motorclub — Track Specifications

## Overview
**"A Multi-Configurable 4 Mile Road Course With Three Unique Tracks"**

FIA rated | All-season | Northeast Florida

---

## Track 1: The Road Course
- **Length:** 2.75 miles (traditional road course)
- **Straightaway:** 4,000-foot main straight
- **Features:** Fast-flowing esses, banked corners, dramatic elevation changes
- **Safety:** Generous runoff areas throughout
- **Character:** Uniquely diverse driving experience — suitable for track days, driver development, time trials, and events

---

## Track 2: The Street Course
- **Length:** 0.76 miles
- **Design:** Street-style road course simulating actual urban circuit driving
- **Safety:** No concrete barriers — designed to test limits safely
- **Use Case:** Car control, street car testing, HPDE, autocross-style events

---

## Track 3: The Ring
- **Format:** Configurable oval track
- **Size:** Expandable from ¼ mile to ½ mile
- **Bonus:** 400-foot skid pad integrated into design
- **Use Case:** Oval technique refinement, car control drills, high-speed confidence building

---

## Combined Configuration
- All three tracks can be used independently or in combination
- ~4 mile total course when fully combined
- Three-tracks-in-one concept maximizes variety and event hosting flexibility

---

## Driving Programs (via SLR / CUBE 3)
- Concierge driving service
- Professional driver coaching
- Driver development programs
- Corporate and private track experiences

---

## Track Infrastructure
- Pit-lane garages
- Paddock-style staging areas
- Professional timing and safety systems (FIA standard)
- Runoff areas at all high-speed corners
