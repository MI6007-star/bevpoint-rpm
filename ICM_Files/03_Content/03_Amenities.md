## Aviation / Fly-In Infrastructure
### Palatka Municipal Airport (Kay Larkin Field)
- **Distance:** Less than 0.1 miles — walking distance
- **Runway:** 6,000 ft (expanding to 7,000 ft)
- **FBO:** Modern, full-service Fixed Base Operator
- **Hangars:** Private aviation hangars directly connected to the property
- **Access:** Fly private → walk/drive to pit lane → hit the track
