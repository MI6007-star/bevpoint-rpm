## Comparable Private Motor Club Developments

### The Benchmark: The Thermal Club (Palm Springs, CA)
- Arguably the gold standard in US private motor clubs
- 4 tracks, garage condos, resort amenities
- Membership in the $100K+ range
- Full sell-out — waiting lists
- Roaring Pines is positioned to be the Southeast/Florida equivalent

### Other Comps to Research
- **Monticello Motor Club** (New York) — private members track, Northeast market

- **Autobahn Country Club** (Chicago) — private members road course
- **Atlanta Motorsports Park** — Southeast comp, different model
- **Circuit of the Americas** (Austin, TX) — public but relevant for Florida cross-market draw

## Why Florida, Why Now
- Florida has no state income tax → attracts high-net-worth relocators
- Post-COVID migration to Florida accelerated dramatically — wealth concentration increasing
- Aviation culture is strong (Florida is top private aviation state)
- Year-round climate = 12 months of usage vs. seasonal northern clubs
- No comparable private motor club in Florida at this tier
- Motorsport + luxury lifestyle trend accelerating (F1 popularity explosion, Drive to Survive effect)
