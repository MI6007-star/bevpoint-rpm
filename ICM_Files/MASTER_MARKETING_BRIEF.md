## THE BIG STORY (Deck Narrative Arc)

### 1. The Moment
Motorsport is having its biggest cultural moment in decades. F1, Drive to Survive, a generation of affluent enthusiasts who grew up gaming and now want the real thing. The demand for authentic, elite, private driving experiences has never been higher — and the supply has never been more limited.

### 2. The Gap
Florida is home to more private aviation aircraft and high-net-worth individuals than almost any state in the nation. Yet it has no private motor club at the level of The Thermal Club, Monticello, or Spring Mountain. That gap is Roaring Pines.

### 3. The Vision
480 acres. Three FIA-rated tracks in one. Fly in on your own plane, park at the on-site FBO, step into your car. Race. Return to your garage loft, shower in the paddock-style clubhouse, dine at the restaurant. Repeat. This is what a motor club looks like when it's built by people who actually drive.

### 4. The Team
The Lagasse family — Scott Sr. and Scott Jr. — bring over a century of combined professional racing experience. Warren Mosler provides financial leadership. CUBE 3 runs world-class motorsport programs. Rimrock Companies delivers proven commercial real estate development. This is an all-star team built for execution.

- 25-member cap makes this an extreme scarcity play — not a mass marketing campaign

Likely prospect pool: 40–75 targeted individuals from Scott's network, Mosler's network, Christie's client roster, IMSA/PCA connections, and Southeast private aviation community.
